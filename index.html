Tips & Advice for Healthy Hair, Brows & Lashes

Be Consistent with Application

For best results, apply Meduza Hair Oil or Brows & Lashes Oil daily. Consistency is key when stimulating natural growth and seeing visible improvements in texture and strength.

Use the Right Amount

A little goes a long way. Use a few drops of oil and gently massage it in with your fingertips. Overapplying can weigh down the hair or lashes.

Apply Before Bedtime

Nighttime is when your body repairs itself. Applying the oils before bed allows the natural ingredients to deeply nourish and activate follicle growth while you sleep.

Massage to Stimulate Circulation

Massaging your scalp or lash line with the oil boosts blood circulation, which encourages healthier and faster hair growth.

Cleanse Before Applying

Always apply the oils on a clean scalp, brow area, or lash line. Removing dirt and makeup ensures maximum absorption and effectiveness.

Avoid Heat Styling Right After Use

Let your hair or lashes absorb the oil fully before using heat tools like straighteners or curlers. Heat can reduce the potency of natural oils.

Hydration is Essential

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Hydrated skin and follicles respond better to topical products like Meduza Oils.

Support with a Healthy Diet

Natural hair and lash growth is also supported by a balanced diet rich in vitamins A, C, E, and biotin. Your beauty starts from within!
